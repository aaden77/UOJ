About

The University of Jamaica - Coastal Campus is a vibrant educational institution located on the picturesque shores of the Caribbean Sea. This campus specializes in marine sciences, environmental studies, and tourism management, offering students hands-on learning experiences through its state-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities. With a commitment to sustainability and community engagement, the Coastal Campus fosters a collaborative environment where students can explore the rich cultural heritage of Jamaica while developing innovative solutions to global challenges. The campus features lush gardens, modern classrooms, and access to pristine beaches, creating an inspiring backdrop for academic and personal growth.
