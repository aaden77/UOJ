Athletics

Welcome to Our Athletics Program!

The University of Jamaica is proud to offer a dynamic athletics program that emphasizes physical fitness, teamwork, and personal development. Our athletic facilities include a modern gymnasium, a multi-sport field, and access to nearby beaches for water sports.

Sports Offered

Track and Field:

Our track and field program includes sprints, middle-distance, long-distance, hurdles, and field events like long jump and shot put. Athletes train year-round to compete in local and national competitions.

Football:

Join our men's and women's Football teams, where players develop their skills through practice and participate in inter-collegiate matches. We focus on teamwork, strategy, and sportsmanship.

Basketball:

Our basketball teams compete in local leagues and tournaments. Training emphasizes skill development, teamwork, and physical fitness. All skill levels are welcome!

Volleyball:

Both indoor and beach volleyball are offered. Our teams participate in regional competitions, focusing on strategy, agility, and teamwork.

Swimming:

Our swimming program caters to all levels, from beginners to advanced swimmers. We participate in various competitions and focus on improving technique and endurance.

Surfing:

With access to beautiful beaches, our surfing program teaches skills for all levels. We organize surf competitions and beach clean-up events.

Tennis:

Our tennis program focuses on skill development and competitive play. Join our teams for inter-collegiate competitions and local tournaments.

Rugby:

Rugby is a growing sport on campus, emphasizing strength, strategy, and teamwork. We participate in friendly matches and tournaments.

Cricket:

Join our cricket team and participate in local leagues. We focus on skill development, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Badminton:

Badminton is a fun and competitive sport offered at our campus. Join our team for training and competitions.

Table Tennis:

Our table tennis program focuses on skill development and competitive play. Join us for practice sessions and tournaments.

Ultimate Frisbee:

Our ultimate frisbee team promotes fitness, teamwork, and fun. We participate in local tournaments and friendly matches.

Coaches

Coach Michael Thompson

Head Coach, Track and Field

Coach Thompson has over 15 years of experience coaching athletes at various levels. He specializes in sprinting and hurdles, focusing on technique and mental preparation.

Coach Robert Johnson

Head Coach, Football

With a background as a collegiate player, Coach Johnson emphasizes teamwork and strategic play. He has led the Men's Football team to multiple championships.

Coach David Lee

Head Coach, Basketball

Coach Lee brings a wealth of knowledge from his professional playing career. He focuses on skill development and creating a strong team culture.

Coach Emily Rivera

Head Coach, Volleyball

Coach Rivera has coached at both the high school and collegiate levels. She is passionate about developing players' skills and fostering a love for the game.

Coach Sarah Mitchell

Head Coach, Swimming

With a background in competitive swimming, Coach Mitchell focuses on technique and endurance training, helping swimmers achieve their personal bests.

Join Our Teams

We encourage all students to participate in athletics, whether as a member of a team or through recreational activities. Trials for teams are held at the beginning of each semester, and all skill levels are welcome.
