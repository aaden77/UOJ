Overview

The University of Jamaica is committed to advancing knowledge and innovation through cutting-edge research. Our research programs are designed to address local and global challenges, fostering a culture of inquiry and collaboration.

Research Areas

Environmental Sciences

Research focused on sustainability, climate change, and biodiversity conservation.

Health and Medicine

Innovative studies in public health, epidemiology, and medical research.

Social Sciences

Exploring societal issues, culture, and human behavior through interdisciplinary research.

Engineering and Technology

Advancements in engineering practices, renewable energy, and technology development.

Arts and Humanities

Research that enriches understanding of cultural heritage and human expression.

Faculty

Our faculty members are leaders in their fields, dedicated to mentoring students and conducting impactful research. They collaborate with national and international partners to enhance research outcomes.

Collaborations

The University of Jamaica partners with various organizations, government agencies, and other academic institutions to promote interdisciplinary research and knowledge transfer.
