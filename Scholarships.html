Overview

The University of Jamaica offers a variety of scholarships to support students in their educational journey. Our scholarships aim to recognize academic excellence and provide financial assistance to those in need.

Types of Scholarships

  • Merit-Based Scholarships: Awarded to students with outstanding academic achievements.
  • Need-Based Scholarships: Designed to assist students with financial difficulties.
  • Sports Scholarships: For students who excel in athletics.
  • Community Service Scholarships: For students who have demonstrated a commitment to community service.
  • Field-Specific Scholarships: Targeted towards students pursuing specific fields of study.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for scholarships, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be a registered student at the University of Jamaica.
  • Maintain a minimum GPA as specified for each scholarship.
  • Submit all required documentation by the application deadline.
  • For need-based scholarships, financial documentation may be required.

Application Process

To apply for a scholarship, please follow these steps:

  1. Review the scholarship options available.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Submit required documents, including transcripts and letters of recommendation.
  4. Await notification of your scholarship status.

Application Deadlines

It is important to adhere to the following deadlines:

  • Fall Semester Scholarships: June 30
  • Spring Semester Scholarships: November 30
  • Summer Scholarships: April 15

International Students

The University of Jamaica welcomes international students and offers specific scholarships designed to support their studies. Eligibility criteria may vary based on the scholarship type.

International students are encouraged to check the requirements and apply early to ensure they meet all deadlines.
